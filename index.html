What a failing website looks like

Most business owners don't know their website is broken. Here's what's silently killing your conversion rate.

🐌 Slow loading

A 1-second delay in load time reduces conversions by 7%. Most small business websites load in 4-8 seconds. Every second is losing you money.

📱 Broken on mobile

Over 60% of web traffic is mobile. If your site doesn't work perfectly on a phone, you're invisible to the majority of your potential clients.

🔍 Invisible to Google

Without proper SEO structure, Google can't rank you. Your competitors show up. You don't. Every day costs you customers you never knew existed.

🔒 Security warnings

An expired SSL certificate makes browsers show a scary warning to visitors. Most leave immediately. Your site may be showing this right now.

📭 No lead capture

97% of visitors leave without contacting you. Without a chatbot or lead form, you'll never know they were there. That's revenue gone forever.

📊 No tracking

If you're not tracking visitor behaviour, you're flying blind. You can't fix what you can't measure — and most businesses measure nothing.
